Frequently Asked Questions:

Are you a GitHub product?

We are NOT by GitHub nor run by GitHub we just use their CSS branding called Primer so you can get a likewise experience you can get with them. (primer.style/css)

How do I sign in?

Once you press sign in it will redirect you to GitHub to authorise the app. If it doesn't please add an issue here.

What is the pricing?

During the Public Beta it will be free then once it comes to a close we may put a pricing plan in place depending on the results.

Will I be paid if I participate?

You will not be compensated but instead will be given one month free once we come out of the beta.

If I need support where do I go?

You can create a issue here where someone will be happy to try and give you an answer.
